Example 4.2.6. The complete graph \(K_5\) isn't planar.

Let's apply the planarity algorithm to the complete graph \(K_5\text{.}\) Let's label the vertices 1-5, and take our Hamiltonian cycle \(C\) to be 123451, which we've drawn as the outside pentagon in the following figure:
